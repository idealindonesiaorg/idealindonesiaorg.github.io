IDEAL Indonesia was born out of the concerns and care of a group of young people who saw the challenges in youth development, especially in education and leadership. These concerns eventually sparked a big idea — to create a platform that supports the creativity, potential, and drive for change among Indonesia’s younger generation.

IDEAL Indonesia was officially established on August 17, 2023. It’s a non-governmental organization under the Ruang Karya Pemuda Nusantara Foundation. The date was chosen intentionally to reflect the spirit of independence and a strong commitment to helping young people overcome barriers in accessing education and building value-based, integrity-driven leadership.

The name “IDEAL” represents a vision of shaping young individuals to be progressive thinkers, solution-oriented doers, and collaborative changemakers. IDEAL Indonesia aims to be both a creative space and a catalyst for youth-led social movements. Through its various development programs focused on collaboration and community service, IDEAL is committed to creating equal opportunities for all Indonesian youth — empowering them to access education and become responsible, inclusive, and impactful future leaders. IDEAL Indonesia believes that the dream of a golden generation by 2045 can only be achieved if today’s youth are genuinely prepared, given room to grow, and actively involved in creating change.
